Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

yhile.com is a free collection of browser-based tools. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how your files are handled.

Your files stay on your device

Every tool on yhile.com runs entirely inside your browser. When you upload an image, video, or audio file, or paste text into a tool, that data is processed locally using browser APIs (such as the Web Audio API, FileReader API, and WebAssembly). Nothing you upload or paste is ever sent to a server — because we don't have one. We have no backend, no database, and no file storage.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (via gtag.js) to understand how visitors use the site — for example, which tools are most popular and how much traffic we receive. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • General location (country/city level, derived from IP address)
  • Browser type and device type
  • How you arrived at the site (search, direct, referral)

This data is collected by Google and processed under Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your individual data — we only see aggregated statistics (e.g. "500 people visited the timer tool this week").

Google Analytics uses cookies to track sessions. By using yhile.com you consent to these cookies. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

We use two types of cookies / local storage:

  • Google Analytics cookies — set by Google to measure site traffic (see above).
  • Cookie consent preference — a small flag stored in your browser's localStorage to remember that you dismissed our cookie banner. This is never sent anywhere.

No accounts, no email, no personal data

yhile.com has no sign-up, no login, and no contact forms that collect personal data. We never ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information. The only contact method is our email address listed on the contact page, which you use voluntarily.

Third-party services

The only third-party service we use is Google Analytics. We do not use any advertising networks, social tracking pixels, affiliate trackers, or other third-party scripts.

Children's privacy

yhile.com does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The site's tools are general-purpose utilities suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

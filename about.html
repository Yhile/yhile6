About yhile.com

yhile.com is a growing collection of free, simple tools built for everyday use. No accounts, no subscriptions, no paywalls — open a tool and use it instantly.

The site exists because too many "free" online tools turn out to require a signup, upload your files to a server, limit what you can do without paying, or show you ads. yhile.com does none of those things. Every tool here is free forever and runs entirely in your browser.

Our principles

🔒

Your files stay on your device

Every tool on yhile.com processes data locally using browser APIs (JavaScript, Canvas, WebAssembly). Nothing is uploaded. We cannot see your files — technically or legally.

🚫

No account required — ever

There is no login, no email, no registration, and no profile to create. You are anonymous by design.

💸

Truly free

Not "freemium." Not a free trial. Not a free tier with limits. Every tool is completely free to use, for everyone, forever — with no premium plan or upgrade required.

🛡️

No tracking or ads

We don't run ads. We don't use tracking cookies. We don't install analytics that follow you across the web. The only data we have is standard server access logs, which we don't use to identify you.

Tools available now

All tools run in any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — on desktop and mobile.
